How to Use WhatsApp for Business Leads

WhatsApp is a powerful tool for business. Add a WhatsApp button to your website, run Facebook ads with a WhatsApp CTA, and automate replies with WhatsApp Business App.

Offer discounts or free quotes via chat. This builds trust and helps you convert visitors into paying customers quickly.
